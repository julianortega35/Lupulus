Frequently asked questions


Top 5 questions people ask about beer


  1. When was beer invented?
    Beer can trace its roots as far back as Mesopotamia – 5th millennium B.C. or older.

  2. How do I make beer?
    With hops, barley and patience!

  3. How many calories are in a beer?
    We could go all day on this one, but a couple examples include: Guinness: 153; Heineken: 150; Corona: 148.

  4. Which country drinks the most beer?
    the Czech Republic consumes 156 liters of beer per capita, more than any other country, followed by Ireland and Germany.

  5. How long does beer stay in your system?
    On average, 6 – 10 hours.
